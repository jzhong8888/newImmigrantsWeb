My Activism Project: The Difficulties Encountered by New Immigrants

The difficulties new immigrants to the United States face and what kind of help they need.

About My Topic

New immigrants to the United States are likely to encounter various problems in economy, language communication, psychology and so on. It takes a long time for new immigrants to adapt to the new life because of their unfamiliarity with the United States and cultural differences. This is likely to be a difficult time for them. Therefore, it is hoped that the public can be more tolerant and offer help to the new immigrants.

The Difficulties Faced by New Immigrants

At School

In addition to language difficulties, many new immigrant students also need to meet new people. This greatly increased the pressure on study and psychology.

At Work

In the workplace, new immigrants need to readjust to a different work culture. Language problems can also be a barrier to finding a job.
